Video Pipeline v6 — Grok Starting Frame + Kling V3 Pro

Two-step process: Grok composites subjects onto a real plate photo (starting frame), then Kling V3 Pro animates that still into a 15-second video. Every frame shows the REAL 515 cabins.

Pipeline (5 Steps)

1. Clean the plate — use Grok image remover to remove obstructions
2. Build starting frame — upload clean plate to Grok, paste composite prompt to add subjects
3. Animate — Kling V3 Pro image-to-video via fal.ai, 15 seconds, single shot
4. Phone camera look — FFmpeg adds subtle grain, softness, desaturation
5. Format overlay — BREAKING NEWS ticker or CCTV timestamp via Pillow + FFmpeg
~$1.68 per video via fal.ai. 9:16 vertical. No audio (add in CapCut).

3 Video Formats

CCTV

Static camera. Timestamp overlay, camera label, REC dot. Phone camera grain masks AI artifacts.

NEWS

BREAKING NEWS ticker (same width as headline box, rounded corners) + headline card. Phone camera shake.

iPHONE

Handheld camera shake, slight grain, cinematic. Best for epic/impossible moments.
