Questions

What exactly happens during the mystery shop?
Our AI calls your business phone number posing as a real customer in your industry. It asks about services, pricing, and availability, just like a real customer would. We grade how your team handles the call across 6 categories and send you a detailed report with the full transcript.
How fast do I get results?
Within 1 hour of payment. The call happens within minutes. Grading and report generation take a few more minutes. Your full report lands in your inbox typically within 30 minutes.
Will my staff know it's a mystery shop?
No. The AI sounds like a real customer asking real questions about your services. Your staff won't know they're being evaluated, which means you get an honest picture of what your customers actually experience.
What if nobody answers?
That's important data. If your business phone goes to voicemail, you'll get an F for Answer Speed and we'll note it in the report. 62% of service businesses miss calls. Better to find out from us for $29 than lose a $1,500 customer.
Is this legal?
Yes. You're authorizing us to call your own business. This is a self-shop, which is standard practice in the mystery shopping industry. The authorization checkbox on the form documents your consent.
Can I use this to shop a competitor?
Currently, Phone Grade is for your own business only. You must be the owner or an authorized manager of the business being called.
What if I want regular mystery shops?
You can order as many Phone Grades as you want. Many business owners do a monthly shop to track improvement. Each shop is a separate $29 purchase.
