THE SALES SEQUENCE

1. THE OPENER

"If I could show you exactly where your business is losing money — for free, takes 60 seconds — would you want to see it?"

If yes → pull up bosssystems.co/analyze.html and walk them through it. If they hesitate → "No pitch, no commitment. It just shows your numbers."

2. DIAGNOSE

Follow up with:

  • "How do you follow up with customers after a job's done?"
  • "What eats the most time but doesn't make you money?"

3. PAINT THE PICTURE

HVAC: "July, 102 degrees, you're on a roof. Phone rings. Voicemail. $1,500 AC job — gone."
Plumber: "Tuesday night, 9 PM, pipe bursts. Voicemail. Emergency markup $400-$800. Gone."
Roofer: "Storm Saturday night. 7 AM Sunday, 15 homeowners calling. First to answer books all 15."

4. THE NUMBER

"That's about $30,000 a month lost to missed calls. Even if we only catch 10%, that's $36,000 back for $600 in annual cost."

5. THE ANALYSIS (your best tool)

"Let me show you — takes 60 seconds. I just need your business type and city."

Pull up bosssystems.co/analyze.html and run it with them live. The numbers do the selling. When they see "$367K lost to missed calls" with their own business type, they close themselves.

6. CLOSE

"I have two build slots this week — Wednesday or Friday. Which works better?"
Close with "which," not "whether." Assume the yes, offer a choice.
"Right now every call that hits voicemail costs you money. You're paying $7,333 a month to NOT answer your phone."

7. THE DEMO CLOSE

"Call this number right now: (903) 522-4459. That's our AI. Talk to it like a customer. If it doesn't blow your mind, I'll leave you alone."
PEAK SEASON (JUNE-AUG) — 15 SECONDS
"I called your business today as a customer and got voicemail. I know you're slammed right now — that's exactly why those calls are going to your competitor. We fix that for fifty bucks a month. Can I set it up this week?"
VOSS TECHNIQUES

ACCUSATION AUDIT

"I know what you're thinking — here's some guy trying to sell you something. You probably think AI can't handle real calls. And $50 sounds too cheap to be real. I get it."

Then silence. 9/10 times: "Well, what is it exactly?"

MIRRORING

Repeat their last 1-3 words as a question:

  • "We're getting by." → "Getting by?"
  • "My wife handles it." → "Your wife handles it?"

NO-ORIENTED QUESTIONS

  • "Would it be ridiculous to try this for a month?"
  • "Have you given up on fixing the phone problem?"
