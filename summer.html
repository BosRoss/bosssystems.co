Real Questions

Things People Actually Ask

What if no calls come in the first week?
The AI answers every call, but first you have to get calls. That means posting on Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, and Nextdoor the day you go live. Most partners get their first job within 48 hours of posting. BOSS Systems gives you the exact posts to use. If you're not willing to post on social in week one, this isn't for you.
Why does BOSS Systems take 20%? That seems like a lot for "just building an app."
You're not paying for an app. You're getting a dedicated phone number, a custom AI agent trained on your business type, automated booking, payment collection, review requests, and ongoing support, with zero upfront cost. BOSS Systems takes a 20% fee and only gets paid if you do. If you think you can build that yourself, go for it. If not, 20% for a free AI-powered business is a good deal.
What if something breaks or the AI says something wrong?
Text Boston at 903-714-6162. BOSS Systems monitors all partner systems and fixes issues same day. The AI is trained specifically on your business type and has been used on real businesses. If it quotes a wrong price or books the wrong thing, we fix the training. You're not on your own.
What if I want to quit?
3 days notice by text. BOSS Systems deactivates the system. No penalty, no hard feelings. The business name and any reviews you built are yours to keep. If you want to continue after summer, you can keep the AI running at standard pricing or just run the business manually without it.
How does BOSS Systems know I'm reporting the right revenue number?
We don't check every transaction. This runs on your word. The AI system logs every booking, which gives a rough cross-check. If the numbers consistently don't match, the agreement ends. Partners who are honest about a bad week get support. Partners who fudge numbers lose the system and the market.
I'm 16 and have never run a business. Can I actually do this?
Yes. That's exactly who this is for. You don't need business experience. You need to be reliable, willing to post on social, and actually show up for jobs. The AI handles everything that requires business knowledge. You just need to do the physical work and answer your phone when Boston texts you.
