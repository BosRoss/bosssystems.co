1. The Parties

This agreement is between Boston Rossall, operating as BOSS Systems ("BOSS Systems"), located in Tyler, Texas, and the individual who has submitted a partner info sheet and received written confirmation from Boston Rossall ("Partner").

This agreement becomes binding when the Partner submits the info sheet with the agreement checkbox checked, or when the Partner confirms acceptance of these terms by text or email to Boston Rossall.

2. What BOSS Systems Provides

BOSS Systems will build and maintain the following for the Partner, at no upfront cost:

  • A dedicated local phone number for the Partner's business
  • An AI voice agent (powered by Retell AI) configured for the Partner's specific business type, trained to answer calls, quote jobs, book appointments, and request reviews
  • An automated post-call workflow for booking confirmations, payment follow-up, and review requests
  • Initial configuration of business name, service area, and pricing based on information provided by the Partner
  • Ongoing support for system issues by text (903-714-6162), with same-day response during normal hours
  • Market exclusivity: BOSS Systems will not build the same business type for a competing partner in the Partner's stated city while the partnership is active

BOSS Systems does not guarantee any specific volume of calls, bookings, or revenue. System availability is targeted at 95%+ uptime, but occasional downtime may occur due to third-party service providers (Retell AI, n8n). Boston will restore service as quickly as possible when notified.

3. What the Partner Is Responsible For

  • Performing all physical services (labor, transportation, equipment, supplies)
  • Marketing the business locally (posting on Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, Nextdoor, and neighborhood groups) to generate call volume
  • Showing up reliably for all booked jobs. Flaking on booked jobs damages the business's reviews and reputation, which affects the Partner's ability to grow
  • Collecting payment from customers (via the payment link the AI sends, or directly)
  • Reporting total gross revenue to Boston by text every Sunday by 8:00 PM CT
  • Paying Boston's 20% revenue share to Venmo @BosRoss by Monday noon of the following week
  • Notifying Boston immediately if the AI system appears to be malfunctioning, quoting incorrect prices, or behaving unexpectedly
  • Not modifying, sharing, or attempting to replicate the AI system configuration

4. Revenue Share

Boston's share: 20% of gross revenue. Gross revenue means the total amount collected from customers before any expenses. The Partner keeps 80%.

Example: Partner completes $3,000 in jobs in one week. Boston's share is $600. Partner keeps $2,400. Partner texts "Week of [date] — $3,000 gross" and Venmos $600 to @BosRoss by Monday noon.

Revenue reporting operates on the honor system. Boston does not audit every transaction, but the AI system logs all bookings, which provides a cross-reference. Partners who consistently underreport or stop reporting will have their system deactivated without further notice.

Weeks with zero revenue require no payment, but the Partner should still text Boston to confirm zero activity.

5. Program Duration and Termination

No fixed end date. This partnership continues until either party terminates it. The program is year-round.

Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with 3 days written notice (text or email). No penalty applies to either party upon termination.

BOSS Systems may terminate this agreement immediately, without notice, if the Partner:

  • Fails to report revenue for two or more consecutive weeks without communication
  • Flakes on three or more booked jobs in a single week
  • Engages in fraudulent, deceptive, or illegal conduct in connection with the business
  • Attempts to circumvent the revenue share arrangement

6. Business Ownership

The business name, any Google Business Profile created, all customer reviews, and the customer relationship belong entirely to the Partner. BOSS Systems has no claim over the business identity or customer base.

The AI phone number, Retell AI configuration, and n8n workflow are property of BOSS Systems and will be deactivated upon termination. The Partner may continue operating the business manually after deactivation.

If the Partner wishes to transition from the 80/20 revenue share to a flat monthly fee, standard BOSS Systems pricing applies: $1,997 build fee waived, $197/month ongoing service fee.
