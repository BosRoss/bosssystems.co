## RULES FOR ALL TEXTS
1. **Never send a text that reads like marketing.** If it has exclamation points, emojis, or "Don't miss out" — rewrite it.
2. **Use their name.** Always.
3. **Specific > generic.** "$340 from 6 jobs" beats "great earnings!"
4. **One text per purpose.** Never stack multiple asks in one message.
5. **No pressure = more responses.** "No pressure either way" gets more replies than "Let me know ASAP."
6. **The wife/spouse text is the most important text in onboarding.** She controls the bank account. She decides if the charge survives month 2.
7. **For teens: "lmaooo" test.** If a teen would forward your text with "is this dumb lol" and their friend would say "nah that's cool" — you're good. If not, rewrite.
