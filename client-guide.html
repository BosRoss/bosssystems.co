How Your AI Handles Calls

When someone calls your AI number, here's exactly what happens:

1
AI Picks Up in Under 3 Seconds
No rings, no hold music, no "press 1 for..." — the AI answers immediately with your business name and greeting.
2
Handles the Conversation
The AI asks what they need, answers questions about your services and pricing, and figures out if they want to book an appointment, get a quote, or just need info.
3
Books the Appointment
If the caller wants to schedule, the AI books it based on your availability. It collects their name, address, and what they need done, then confirms the date and time.
4
You Get the Details
Every call — booked, inquiry, callback request — shows up on your dashboard. You can see exactly what was said, what was booked, and who called. Nothing falls through the cracks.

After hours, weekends, holidays — it doesn't matter. Your AI answers every call the same way, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The calls that come in at 10pm on a Saturday? Those are the ones your competitors lose to voicemail. You don't.

Common Questions

Do callers know they're talking to an AI?
The AI identifies itself upfront — this is required by federal law. But it sounds natural and conversational, not robotic. Most callers don't care as long as their question gets answered and their appointment gets booked.
What if a caller asks something the AI doesn't know?
The AI will let them know it doesn't have that information and offer to have someone call them back. It never makes up answers. The call still shows up on your dashboard so nothing gets lost.
Can I still answer my own phone?
Absolutely. Most clients set up call forwarding so the AI only picks up when they can't — like when they're on a job, driving, or after hours. If you answer first, the AI never rings. You can also turn forwarding on and off whenever you want.
What if I need to block a specific day or week?
Text Boston: "Block out [dates]." The AI will stop booking during those times and tell callers your next available date. When you're back, text "I'm available again" and it picks right back up.
How do I set up call forwarding?
On most phones, go to Settings > Phone > Call Forwarding and enter your AI phone number (it's on your dashboard). Or dial *72 then your AI number from your business line. To turn it off, dial *73. If you need help, text Boston and he'll walk you through it for your specific phone.
What happens if I want to cancel?
There's no contract. Text Boston "cancel" and you're done. No fees, no hassle. Your AI number and data stay available for 30 days in case you change your mind.
Is my data private?
Yes. Only you can access your dashboard with your unique code. Your call data, transcripts, and customer information are never shared with anyone else. Boston can see your data for support purposes only.
Can the AI handle multiple calls at the same time?
Yes. Unlike a human receptionist, your AI can handle unlimited simultaneous calls. Two people call at 7pm on a Friday? Both get answered instantly.
