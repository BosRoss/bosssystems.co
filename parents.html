What the Program Is

  • A real summer business. Your teen picks a business type (lawn care, pet sitting, car detailing, tutoring, etc.), and I build the full tech system — phone number, booking, payment collection, review requests.

  • Not a job — there's no boss, no schedule, no W-2. Your teen sets their own hours and picks their own clients.

Questions I Get From Parents

Yes. The program accepts ages 14+. Your teen runs their own business as a sole proprietor — this is the same legal structure as a kid mowing lawns or babysitting, just with better tools. We don't employ your teen — they're an independent operator. Texas labor law permits minors to operate their own businesses. That said, I require your approval before activating anything for anyone under 18.
