SECTION 1
Relationship
Representative is an independent contractor, not an employee, partner, or co-owner of BOSS Systems. Representative is not entitled to employee benefits, workers' compensation, unemployment insurance, health insurance, or any other employee benefits. Representative is solely responsible for their own taxes, including self-employment tax. A 1099-NEC will be issued for earnings over $600 in any calendar year.
Nothing in this agreement creates an employer-employee relationship, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship beyond what is explicitly stated here.
SECTION 2
Representative's Duties
Representative agrees to:
  1. Identify, contact, and close sales of BOSS Systems products and services
  2. Represent BOSS Systems honestly and professionally at all times
  3. Follow the pricing, product descriptions, and sales guidelines provided by the Company
  4. Send accurate client information to the Company immediately after each closed sale (name, business, phone, email, services needed)
  5. Collect payment from clients via the Company's designated payment method (currently Venmo @BosRoss) and ensure funds are sent directly to the Company — never to the Representative's own account
  6. Disclose that AI-generated voice calls are AI-generated at the start of every call, per FTC 2024 rules
  7. Follow all Do Not Call regulations and TCPA rules — never call numbers on the National DNC Registry, never call before 9 AM or after 6 PM in the prospect's time zone
SECTION 3
Authority and Limits
Representative may NOT:
  • Offer discounts, custom pricing, or modified terms without prior written approval from the Company (text message counts as written)
  • Make guarantees about specific financial outcomes, revenue, or savings for any client
  • Make promises about features, timelines, or capabilities beyond what is in the official Sales Playbook
  • Enter into any contract, agreement, or binding commitment on behalf of BOSS Systems
  • Represent themselves as an employee, partner, owner, or officer of BOSS Systems
  • Accept cash, check, or any payment on behalf of BOSS Systems into their own accounts
  • Hire, subcontract, or delegate their sales duties to any third party without written approval
Any sale made outside these terms — unauthorized discounts, unapproved promises, or misrepresentation — may void the commission on that sale and be grounds for immediate termination.

SECTION 5
Commission Structure
20% commission on all revenue from clients the Representative personally closes.
Setup fees: Representative earns 20% of the one-time setup/build fee collected from each client they close.
Monthly recurring: Representative earns 20% of the monthly recurring fee from each client they close, for as long as the client remains active, paying, and the Representative remains in good standing under this agreement. See Section 11 for what happens after termination.
BOSS OWN clients: Representative earns 20% of the Company's 15% revenue share from BOSS OWN clients they close, for as long as the client remains active.
SPP referral bonus: $20 per Summer Partner Program referral who achieves their first profit, provided the referred partner listed the Representative's name on their application.

SECTION 6
Payment Terms
  1. Setup commissions are paid within 7 business days of the Company receiving full payment from the client.
  2. Monthly recurring commissions are paid on the 15th of each month for the prior month's collections.
  3. Payment method: Venmo, or another method agreed in writing.
  4. Minimum payout: None. All earned commissions are paid regardless of amount.
  5. Clawback: If a client requests a refund, disputes a charge, or reverses payment within 30 days of the sale, the commission for that sale will be deducted from the Representative's next payment. If no future payment is owed, the Representative agrees to return the commission within 14 days of written notice.
  6. Records: The Company will provide a monthly commission statement showing all active clients, payments received, and commissions owed. Representative may request this at any time.
SECTION 7
Client Attribution
A sale is attributed to the Representative only if both of the following are true:
  1. The Representative made the first meaningful sales contact with the prospect, AND
  2. The prospect was not already in the Company's pipeline, previously contacted by the Company, or an inbound lead generated by the Company's own marketing
If there is a dispute about who originated a lead, the Company's CRM/pipeline records are the final authority.
The Representative does not own the client relationship after the sale. Post-sale support, billing, retention, and technical delivery are handled exclusively by the Company.
SECTION 8
Non-Circumvention
Representative agrees to never circumvent BOSS Systems by dealing directly with any client, prospect, vendor, or partner that was introduced through BOSS Systems. This means:
  • Never accept payment from a BOSS client directly (all payments go through the Company)
  • Never offer a BOSS client alternative services that bypass the Company
  • Never redirect a BOSS prospect to a competing service provider
  • Never encourage a BOSS client to cancel their service and re-sign directly with the Representative or a third party
Violation of this section is grounds for immediate termination, forfeiture of all unpaid commissions, and may result in legal action for damages.
SECTION 9
Non-Solicitation
During this agreement and for 12 months after termination, Representative will not:
  1. Solicit, recruit, or attempt to redirect any BOSS Systems client to a competing service
  2. Contact any BOSS Systems client for the purpose of selling competing AI phone, automation, or receptionist services
  3. Use BOSS Systems' client lists, contact information, or internal data to benefit a competing business
This does not prevent the Representative from:
  • Working any other job, in any other industry
  • Selling non-competing products or services
  • General networking, social media, or relationship building
  • Responding if a former client contacts them independently for non-competing matters
SECTION 10
Confidentiality
Representative agrees to keep strictly confidential:
  • Client information (names, contact details, account details, revenue, call data)
  • Company pricing strategies, cost structures, margins, and vendor agreements
  • Proprietary technology, AI systems, workflows, prompts, and business processes
  • Internal sales playbooks, training materials, and dashboard tools
  • Commission rates and internal financial information
  • Any login credentials, API keys, or system access provided
  • Any information marked or reasonably understood to be confidential
Representative may share product information, pricing, and marketing materials that are already publicly available on bosssystems.co.
This obligation survives termination of this agreement indefinitely.
SECTION 11
Termination
At-will: Either party may terminate this agreement at any time, for any reason, with 7 days written notice. Text message or email counts as written notice.
Immediate termination: The Company may terminate immediately, without notice, for any of the following:
  • Fraud, dishonesty, or misrepresentation
  • Circumventing the Company (Section 8)
  • Unauthorized discounts or promises to clients
  • Breach of confidentiality
  • Conduct that damages the Company's reputation
  • Accepting client payments into personal accounts
  • Violation of any law in connection with sales activities
After termination — what happens to commissions:
If terminated in good standing (no cause, either party just decides to part ways):
• Setup commissions on deals already closed: paid in full
• Monthly recurring commissions: continue for 90 days after the termination date, then stop
• SPP referral bonuses already earned: paid in full
If terminated for cause (fraud, circumvention, breach of confidentiality, etc.):
• All unpaid commissions are forfeited
• Monthly recurring commissions stop immediately
• Company reserves the right to seek damages
Upon termination, Representative must immediately:
  1. Stop representing themselves as affiliated with BOSS Systems
  2. Return or delete all Company materials, client data, and confidential information
  3. Remove any BOSS Systems branding from personal profiles or materials
