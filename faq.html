How does AI phone answering work?
When a customer calls your business number, the AI answers immediately — 24/7, including nights, weekends, and holidays. It greets them by your business name, answers their questions using information you provide, books appointments, and sends you a text summary after every call.

If the AI doesn't know the answer, it tells the caller it will have someone get back to them and sends you an immediate notification with the caller's question and phone number so you can follow up.

Any service business that gets phone calls: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, lawn care, pest control, auto repair, dental, legal, and more. If you miss calls and lose jobs because of it, this is built for you.

No. We give you a phone number that forwards to the AI. You can set your existing business number to forward to it when you can't answer, or use the new number directly. Works with any phone system — no apps, no hardware, no IT.
